🎮 How to Play (highlights)

    📝 About This Game

    Rewilding the Future is an interactive simulation where you restore Pleistocene-style ecosystems across Europe by reintroducing species, balancing predator-prey dynamics, and raising ecosystem health.

    🌍 What Is Rewilding?

    Rewilding restores degraded landscapes by reintroducing keystone species, reconnecting habitats, and letting nature’s own processes drive recovery—boosting biodiversity, climate resilience, and human-nature connections.

    🎥 About My YouTube Channel

    I’m the creator of Rewilding the Future on YouTube, using AI voice-overs plus research from books, scientific articles, and AI to bring real rewilding projects to life.

    🎮 How to Play

    1. Select Kids, Youth, or Explorer mode.
    2. Click a map marker to open its reserve and read its description.
    3. Filter Animals/Plants, click a species, and choose how many to release (1–100).
    4. Watch your ecosystem health bar rise and fall as you “Next Day.”
    5. Every 5 days you’ll get a mini-quiz—correct answers +5 health!
    6. Earn achievements (badges & confetti) as you hit milestones.

    🎚️ Mode Descriptions

    • Kids: Simple UI, big confetti, easy steps.
    • Youth: Full basic species, one-paragraph bios, moderate pace.
    • Explorer: Every species & plant, full bios, detailed summaries.

      • 🏘️ Community & Infrastructure

      • 💚 Nature Keepers, Community Members boost health.
      • 👮 Police cut poacher risk; 🚒 Firefighters cut fire risk.
      • 🛎️ Tour Guides earn money but stress wildlife (–health).
      • 🏨 Hotels & 🍴 Restaurants make daily revenue (and a small health bonus).
      • Manage your budget (start with $1 000)!
